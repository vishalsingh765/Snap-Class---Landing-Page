🎯Step-by-Step

How SnapClass Works

See the platform in action. Click any image to view it in full screen.

01

Secure Role Portal

Authentication

Users choose their role. The system routes teachers to the management dashboard and students to their tracking portal, protected by secure login.

02

Student Enrollment Dashboard

Onboarding

Students can quickly view their enrolled subjects, check their overall attendance percentage, and input new course codes to join classes dynamically.

03

Course Management

Teacher Tools

Faculty members have full control. They can create new subjects, generate unique shareable codes, and track total student enrollments in one view.

04

AI Attendance Capture

Computer Vision

The core engine. Teachers select the subject and activate the camera. The AI scans the classroom frame, detects registered faces, and maps them to student IDs.

05

Real-time Recognition Results

Processing

In milliseconds, the system outputs verification bounding boxes. It categorises students as Present or Absent with high-confidence facial embeddings.

06

Historical Data & Exports

Analytics

All attendance logs are securely saved with timestamps. Teachers can review past classes, track trends, and export pristine CSV/Excel sheets for administration.
